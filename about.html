"What I cannot create, I do not understand"

- Richard Feynman

This page exists as the best way of verifying one's knowledge is to explain it. I also want to store all my ideas and experiments in one place.

On me - my primary want-to-be is to ask questions about state-of-the-art and solve interesting problems. As a hobby - doing music. I like patterns. I like delving deep. Solving problems (e.g. asking non-standard questions and proving their merit) and music (either listening or playing) are probably two most pleasurable activities.

I am not bullshit-intolerant, but I try to simplify as much as I can. Good things are simple. If they are complicated, they are good if made from simple things. I am quite understanding, because understanding is better than pretty much any fight as usually "people know better".

I dislike assuming that one knows best unless the facts speak for themselves. However hybris is basis to actually knowing best in the future, so I would actually encourage people to think they may know better, but not claim it before proven right.

People change, depending on many factors. One unchanging thing is that people change. Expect this page to change as well.
